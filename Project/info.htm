Google is Evil. A lot of today's technology contains malicious features that users are either unaware of, or hides beneath the surface of the software. Special interest groups and governments collect information about computer users either to analyze their web-behavior for use in using targeted advertisements. Some may insist that the convenience that these products and services provide outweighs the negative aspects and features. Those who agree with this sentiment may see the other side if they consider the practices and publicly-known policies of various technology companies.
